Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, HopeBridge is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Yes, you can pause, edit, or cancel your recurring donation at any time from your Donor Dashboard.

How do I know my money is being used correctly?

We post proof for every completed project, including photos, GPS coordinates, and cost breakdowns.

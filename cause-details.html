Water

Clean Water for Kenya

Turkana, Kenya 15 Days Left
$15,400 raised of $20,000

In the arid region of Turkana, women and children walk over 10 kilometers daily just to fetch water. Often, this water is contaminated, leading to preventable diseases.

HopeBridge is partnering with local engineers to drill 10 solar-powered boreholes. These wells will provide safe, clean, and endless water to 5,000 villagers, drastically reducing disease and allowing children to return to school instead of walking for water.

The Solution

Solar-powered pumps differ from traditional hand-pumps. They are automated, require less physical labor, and fill overhead tanks that distribute water to tap stands throughout the village.

  • Deep Aquifer Access: Drilling 150m deep to reach pure water.
  • Solar Energy: Zero running costs for the community.
  • Sustainability: Local water committees trained for maintenance.

"Before the well, my children were always sick. Now, they are healthy and in school. Water is life."

— Amina, Village Elder

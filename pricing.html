Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will apply at your next billing cycle. No penalties or hidden fees.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new members. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer family plans?

Absolutely! We offer family plans with up to 20% discount for additional family members. Contact our team for custom family package options tailored to your needs.

Is insurance accepted?

We work with most major insurance providers. Many of our services may be partially or fully covered. Our billing team will help you understand your coverage and maximize your benefits.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes, you can pause your membership for up to 3 months per year. Perfect for vacations, medical situations, or life changes. Your plan benefits will resume when you return.

Our Roots

It Started with a Single Well.

2015: The Catalyst

Our founder, Jonathan, witnessed the water crisis in Turkana firsthand. HopeBridge was born in a kitchen with $5,000.

2018: The 100% Model

We transitioned to our radical 100% model, securing private donors to cover all overhead costs forever.

2022: Global Expansion

Reached our 1,000th completed project milestone, expanding across 10 countries in Africa and Asia.

Why We Exist

The Radical Transparency Philosophy

Most NGOs are black boxes. We are a greenhouse. We believe that by showing every detail, we build the trust necessary for true global change.

01. The Donor's Eye

You shouldn't need a magnifying glass to see your impact. We provide GPS coordinates, satellite imagery, and live project updates for every donation.

02. Accountability First

We don't just report successes; we report challenges. If a well breaks, we tell you, and then we show you exactly how we're fixing it.
